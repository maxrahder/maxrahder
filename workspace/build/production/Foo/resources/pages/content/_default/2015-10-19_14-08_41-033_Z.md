#Arithmetic

Arithmetic operators may be used with numeric values:
<code>+ - * /</code>

<pre class="runnable">
Ext.define('MyApp.view.Main', {
    extend: 'Ext.Component',
    xtype: 'main',

    data: {
        name: 'Homer Simpson',
        age: 38
    },
    tpl: '&lt;h1>{name} will be {age + 3} in three years.&lt;/h1>'

});
Ext.application({
    name: 'MyApp',
    mainView: 'MyApp.view.Main'
});</pre>
